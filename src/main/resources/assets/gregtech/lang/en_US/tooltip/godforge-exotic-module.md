The fourth and final module of the Godforge, this module breaks apart the very building blocks of matter, producing exotic mixtures in the process. Quark-Gluon Plasma can be manufactured right away, but production of Magnetic Monopole Matter (Magmatter) requires a fully upgraded Godforge
This module is specialized towards acquisition of unique materials
